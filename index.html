About Me

My name is Kate. I am a Web Developer. I live most of the year in Los Gatos, California. I am an Australian (born and bred) but I have a wanderust to see as much of the world as I can. I love the Northern California weather, diversity and culture, and feel so blessed to reside here. Coding, travelling and cooking are my three favourite ways to spend my time. Else reading/learning about these three things, or fitting in some cycling or tennis or yoga into my days.
